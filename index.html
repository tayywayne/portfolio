I'm Taylor. I'm a software enginear, creative thinker, and a problem solver.

Ever since I discovered geocities I have been in love with creating for the web. As a kid I was always fascinated by technology and programming, my hobby was building layouts and fun sites.

Fastforward into 2021 as I moved into a journey of self improvement, I rediscovered my love of programming and web development. I saw how this field excited me in a way my previous carreer path just didnt. And after practicing some basic HTML/CSS/JS outside of work, I decided in 2022 that I needed to 100% dive in and commit to a new path, one on the web.

Nothing has ever filled me with so much pride and fullfillment the way programming has. What excited me about this work is that I get to stretch my creativity, flex my problem solving, and always has something new to learn.
